The Diamond Legacy

Seven diamonds, Seven colours, seven friends, and seven rings.

======

The Order of the Diamond has chosen the new Diamond Legacy to protect and defend earth as a new threat rises, aiming to take and combine the rings to achieve godlike abilities. Despite the new guardians being goofy or immature, They must fight for their lives to protect the world from this threat.
